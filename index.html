Font

Family

For English："Helvetica Family".
For Japanese："Hiragino Kaku Gothic Pro" , "Yu Gothic" , "meiryo".
For Twian："hanhei TC" , "MSJH.TTF" (Microsoft) , "PingFang TC" (Apple)
For Monospace font："Consolas", "Courier New", monospace, "Osaka", "MS Gothic"
Or All language use "Google Noto Fonts Family" (Free Font)
（This page use "Noto Fonts"）
